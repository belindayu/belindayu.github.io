About me

I have always found it fascinating as to why we appeal to certain layouts, structures, and colors more than others, in addition to the complex design process that goes into building the seemingly ordinary things we use everyday. I first discovered that there was a procedure for this through UX case studies on Medium. I still continue to learn about it today, in addition to the multitude of topics on there.

I knew I enjoyed contributing work for the visual side of projects, whether it was a flyer for a club event or presentation slides for a class, so that it was user-friendly, engaging, and visually pleasing.

So, I took this as a sign that front-end development would be an ideal path for me. During my winter break, I finally took a leap on a HTML/CSS course and love it! I now view every website I go on differently, knowing that someone thoughtfully designed its layout and components for a reason.

I think it's pretty cool that I can build an entire website from scratch that many people will see and use. I'm eager to apply my knowledge and learn about how it all works!
