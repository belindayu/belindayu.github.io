About me

üìç A journey:

My interest in front-end development started in high school, contributing work to the visual side of projects, whether it was a flyer for a club event or presentation slides for a class. I liked having a role in what the audience first sees and hoped to make it engaging and visually pleasing.

So, this made me wonder why we are more drawn to certain layouts, structures, and colors than others. Upon discovering UX, I finally got to learn about the complex design process that goes into building the seemingly ordinary things we use everyday.

With coding, I get to combine both of these interests and make things that other people will see and use. In building a website for the first time, I now view every website I go on differently, knowing that someone thoughtfully designed its layout and components for a reason.

I think it's pretty cool that I can build an entire website from scratch that many people will see and use. I'm eager to apply my knowledge and learn about how it all works!
